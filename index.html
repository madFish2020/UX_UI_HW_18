The Heart of Texas Lab rescue is a non-profit 51(c)(3), all volunteer organization based in Austin, Texas. Our mission is to rescue Labrador Retrievers from shelters, pounds, abandonment, and other situations, and place them in loving, caring homes. The HOT Lab Rescue serves Austin, San Antonio and surrounding areas.

Want to help? Make a donation!

Now that we have our very own ranch, we are in the process of building a climate-controlled kennel building for our dogs. Please donate to help us complete this project.
