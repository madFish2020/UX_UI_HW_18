Heart of Texas Lab Rescue

The heart of Texas Lab Rescue is a non-profit 501(c3), all volnteer organization based in Austin,Texas. Our mission is to rescue Labrador Retrievers from shelters, pounds, abandonment and other situations, and place them in loving, caring homes. The HOT Lab Rescue serves Austin, San Antonio, and surrounding areas.

Want to help? Make a donation!

Not that we have our very own ranch, we are in the process of building a climate-controlled kennel building four our dogs. Please donate to help us complete this project.
